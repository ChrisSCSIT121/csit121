The Fighting Game Glossary

What is this site?

The Fighting Game Glossary as the name would suggest is a glossary for
common and niche terminology from a variety of fighting games. The target
audience for this site are fighting game players of any skill level who want
to learn more about fighting games. The cite also includes video examples
along with the terms.

Organization

Fighting Game Glossary is a hierarchicaly organizaed site that is easy to
understand at first glance. The first thing to catch your eye will likely be
the search bar in at the center of the screne that allows users to quickley
find terms they're looking for. Below the search bar are two filter options.
One filters by starting letter and the other filters by game franchises each
of which has their own unique logo. This also helps the useablity of the
cite.

The cite also makes sure to Contrast terminology from normal text
as all terms are in big bold orange text. Terms are even labled from
which franchise they orgiginated from or are commonly used in.
In case the brightness on the site is too little or too much there's
a light and dark mode toggle to the right of the serach bar.

Audit Score

This personally surprised me but The Fighting Game Glossay scored
a low 38 on the accessibility Checker. The low score is because of a
lack of accessibility options for blind, deaf, and low vision users.

Conclusion

The goal of The Fighting Game Glossary is to be an easy to access and
simple to use source for fighting game terminology and it does a great
job at being just that. The layout is simple and intuitive and terms are
never more than a click and a scroll away from being found. One of
my favorite details of the cite is the inclusion of video examples as
it both helps in building better understanding of a term and is also a
way to keep users engaged with the cite and the information being
presented. Something I would like to see improved about the cite apart
from accessbility for blind and deaf people is allowing users to use
both filter options at the same time as currently it's only filter by letter
or filter by game franchise.

Overall, The Fighting Game Glossary is a fantastic cite that I would
recommend to anyone wanting to learn more about fighting games.
